Your Companion In Staying Fresh

About Us

OPTUS - established in 2019 is an Indian brand of finest kitchenware committed to protect our natural environment by providing alternatives to single use. We strongly believe that recycling is the only way to make the planet sustainable. We envisage a refill reuse culture with healthy, on-toxic products that are highly durable.

Our target are those masses who are looking for contributing and doing their bit in making this planet greener along with keeping style and elegant persona in check. We at OPTUS cater world class designs to give a unique experience of hydrating.

Our focus is to customize in order to offer uniquely light weight collections of bottles and accessories, so as to deliver a more sustainable and comfortable lifestyle. In today's fast paced life it is extremely significant to drink fresh to stay healthy. These thoughts strongly justifies our tag line ''Your Companion In Staying Fresh'' and hence OPT-US.

Our Mission

OPTUS is an Indian brand of finest kitchenware aiming to reduce the impact of disposable plastic bottles on the planet and our lives. The idea is to replace the plastic waste by entirely healthy, elegant and ecological solution and hence meeting the need to hydrate.

We design products that are worth using and indeed making this world a more pleasant and less polluted place. The type of designs that we create at OPTUS will help you to enhance your health and living standard. In addition, OPTUS bottles also improve people's look in an environmentally friendly way.
